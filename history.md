# What is TABConf?

TABConf is an event focused on building the Bitcoin community, providing education, and supporting developers. We aim to be an inclusive, organic, grassroots conference for Bitcoin.

[Michael Tidwell](https://twitter.com/miketwenty1) and [Brandon Iglesias](https://twitter.com/corptostartups) saw an opportunity to further Bitcoin education in Atlanta by bringing great minds together — a stress-free, low-cost conference that delivered real value.

In **2018**, the first TABConf was held at General Assembly in Ponce City Market. Tickets for the one-day event were $10. Within a week, **210 tickets** sold out and the venue was overbooked — setting the trajectory for Bitcoin in Atlanta.

By **2019**, the conference had grown to **500+** attendees at Georgia State University, with four ballrooms running simultaneously so people could choose the content that fit them.

After **2020** threw curveballs for everyone, TABConf returned in **2021** with a sharper focus on attendees and the broader Bitcoin development community. Borrowing from [DEF CON](https://defcon.org/)’s village model, TABConf introduced **Villages** — space for individuals, groups, and communities to run their own programming. TABConf also pledged to donate a percentage of ticket proceeds to support Bitcoin development.

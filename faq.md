# Frequently asked questions

### When is TABConf 8?

**October 12–15, 2026.**

### What’s the schedule breakdown?

- **October 12–13** — Builder Days &amp; workshops.  
- **October 14–15** — Conference sessions (villages / main programming).  

See **[Venue &amp; format]({{ '/venue/' | relative_url }})** and **[Schedule]({{ '/schedule/' | relative_url }})**.

### Where is TABConf 8?

**Georgia Tech Exhibition Hall** in Atlanta, Georgia — same venue family as [TABConf 7](https://7.tabconf.com/faq). Map and context: **[Venue]({{ '/venue/' | relative_url }})**.

### Where should I stay?

See **[Hotels]({{ '/hotels/' | relative_url }})** for options near the venue (based on our TABConf 7 hotel list). Book directly with hotels; blocks and rates vary by year.

### Is TABConf for me?

If you want to go deeper on **how Bitcoin works** — protocol, applications, security, tooling — yes. TABConf is technical, but people learn from each other at many levels.

### How do I propose a talk or workshop?

Open a **[GitHub issue in the TABConf 8 repo](https://github.com/TABConf/8.tabconf.com/issues)**. If a slot is accepted, it can be tracked on the org’s project boards and **[live schedule](https://schedule.tabconf.com/)**. Details: **[Submit a talk]({{ '/submit/' | relative_url }})**.

### Can I buy a single-day ticket?

TABConf typically offers **one ticket type** that covers the full event (Builder Days through the last session). No VIP tiers — see **[tickets on the home page]({{ '/' | relative_url }}).**

### Refunds and transfers

Policies are set per edition. For transfers or questions, email **[hello@tabconf.com](mailto:hello@tabconf.com)** (same channel we used for [TABConf 7](https://7.tabconf.com/faq)).
